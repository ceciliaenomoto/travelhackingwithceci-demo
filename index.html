Quick Guide to Starting with Points and Miles

  1. Choose a Rewards Program
    • Research airline and hotel loyalty programs that fit your travel goals.
    • Consider flexible point currencies like Chase Ultimate Rewards or American Express Membership Rewards.
  2. Sign Up for a Rewards Credit Card
    • Look for a card with a strong sign-up bonus to jumpstart your points balance.
    • Check the annual fee, earning rates, and transfer partners before applying.
  3. Focus Your Spending
    • Use your rewards card for everyday purchases to earn points regularly.
    • Pay attention to bonus categories like dining, groceries, or travel for faster earning.
  4. Learn to Transfer and Redeem
    • Transfer points to airline and hotel partners to unlock premium cabins or upscale accommodations.
    • Redeem wiselyâ€”compare the cash price versus the points cost to find the best value.
  5. Stay Organized
    • Keep track of your points, expiration dates, and upcoming trips.
    • Check promotion emails, loyalty program offers, and travel blogs for insider tips.
